Section 01

Policy Overview

KOLOMIETSSA is committed to providing high-quality digital educational products. We stand behind everything we offer and aim to ensure every client receives genuine value. This Refund Policy sets out the specific conditions under which refunds are granted for each product category.

Because all KOLOMIETSSA products are delivered digitally, special rules apply. The following summary provides a quick-reference guide:

Academy Courses
7-day satisfaction window
Full refund if less than 20% of course consumed and requested within 7 days of purchase

Section 02

Digital Courses — Academy

KOLOMIETSSA Academy courses are individual digital products delivered with immediate access upon payment confirmation. The following refund terms apply.

2.1 Standard 7-Day Satisfaction Period

If you are not satisfied with an Academy course purchase, you may request a full refund within 7 calendar days of your purchase date, provided that:

  • Your refund request is submitted within 7 calendar days of the original purchase date;
  • You have consumed less than 20% of the total course content (measured by video completion percentage tracked within the Platform);
  • You have not downloaded a significant portion of the accompanying course materials (including PDFs, templates, or resource files);
  • You have not used the course content to participate in any live sessions, group calls, or workshops included with the course;
  • Your account is in good standing and has not previously received a refund for the same or related course material.

2.2 Bundle Purchases

For multi-course bundle purchases (including the All 7 Courses package), the 7-day refund window applies to the bundle as a whole. Partial refunds for individual courses within a bundle are not available unless the bundle is priced at the sum of individual course prices, in which case the standard per-course terms apply.

2.3 Course Retirement

If KOLOMIETSSA permanently retires a course you have purchased and does not provide an equivalent replacement, you are entitled to a pro-rata credit or, if requested, a full refund regardless of consumption level, provided you have held the course for fewer than 12 months.

Waiver of Withdrawal Right: By purchasing a digital course and confirming at checkout that delivery commences immediately, EU consumers acknowledge they waive their 14-day statutory right of withdrawal under Directive 2011/83/EU. Our 7-day satisfaction period is a separate voluntary consumer-friendly commitment that functions independently of this waiver.

Section 03

Club Subscriptions

Club subscriptions operate on a recurring billing model. The following refund terms govern subscription payments:

3.1 Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly Club subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing period has commenced. You may cancel at any time and retain access to Club features until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused days within a monthly billing cycle.

3.2 Annual Subscriptions — 30-Day Refund Window

Annual Club subscriptions carry a 30-calendar-day full refund window from the date of annual payment. To qualify for a full annual subscription refund, your request must be:

  • Submitted within 30 calendar days of the annual payment date;
  • The first refund request for that annual subscription period.

After the 30-day window, annual subscriptions are non-refundable for the remainder of the annual term. You may still cancel to prevent future renewal charges.

3.3 Subscription Upgrades & Downgrades

Subscription tier upgrades take effect immediately, with the price difference billed on a pro-rata basis for the remainder of the current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing period. Credit for the difference is applied to the next invoice.

3.4 Free Trial Periods

Where a free trial period is offered, no charge is made until the trial expires. If you cancel before the trial end date, you will not be charged. KOLOMIETSSA reserves the right to limit free trial eligibility to one per person.

Section 04

Consulting & Individual Services

Consulting services, including personal mentorship packages, wealth management consultations, individual coaching sessions, and bespoke strategy services, are subject to separate cancellation and refund terms specified in the individual service agreement.

General Consulting Refund Principles

  • Cancellation before commencement: A full refund is available if the service is cancelled no less than 48 hours before the scheduled first session or delivery date;
  • Cancellation within 48 hours: A 50% cancellation fee applies if the service is cancelled within 48 hours of the scheduled start;
  • Post-commencement: Once a consulting session has taken place or a deliverable has been prepared, the associated fees are non-refundable;
  • Package services: For multi-session packages, unused sessions may be refunded on a pro-rata basis if cancelled within the package validity period, subject to a 15% administration fee.
Section 05

Technical Failures & Service Disruptions

If you are unable to access purchased content due to a demonstrable technical failure on KOLOMIETSSA's side (not attributable to your own device, browser, or internet connection), we will:

  1. Prioritise restoring your access as quickly as technically possible;
  2. Where access cannot be restored within 5 business days, offer a full refund for the affected product regardless of consumption level;
  3. Extend subscription periods to compensate for verified downtime exceeding 48 continuous hours.

Technical issues must be reported to [email protected] with sufficient detail to allow diagnosis. KOLOMIETSSA is not responsible for access issues attributable to your own equipment, browser incompatibility, internet service provider, or regional network restrictions.

Section 06

Non-Refundable Items

The following purchases are non-refundable under any circumstances other than those explicitly described elsewhere in this policy:

  • Completed consulting sessions or coaching calls, regardless of the outcome;
  • Course purchases where more than 20% of the content has been accessed or where the 7-day window has passed;
  • Monthly subscription fees for any portion of a billing period that has already commenced;
  • Annual subscription fees after the 30-day refund window has expired;
  • Promotional, discounted, or flash-sale purchases where a non-refundable condition was explicitly stated at checkout;
  • Add-on purchases, digital downloads, or supplementary materials purchased separately from core course or subscription products.
Section 07

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

01
Email Our Support Team
Send a refund request to [email protected] from the email address registered to your account. Include your order number, the product(s) for which you are requesting a refund, and a brief reason for your request.
02
Review & Verification
Our team will review your request against the applicable policy terms within 3 business days. We may request additional information if necessary to process your request.
03
Refund Decision
You will receive written confirmation of the refund decision via email. If approved, the refund will be initiated via Stripe to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.
04
Funds Return
Refund processing times vary by payment method and card issuer. Stripe typically processes refunds within 5–10 business days. Bank processing time may add an additional 2–5 business days.
Section 08

Stripe Payment Processing

All refunds are processed through Stripe, the payment processor used for all KOLOMIETSSA transactions. Upon approval of your refund request:

  • Stripe will reverse the charge to your original payment method;
  • You will receive a Stripe-generated refund confirmation to your registered email address;
  • Refunds can only be issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase;
  • If the original payment method is no longer valid, our team will work with you to arrange an alternative solution.

Currency: Refunds are processed in the original transaction currency (EUR). If your bank account is in a different currency, exchange rate fluctuations between the date of purchase and the date of refund may result in a different amount being received in your local currency. KOLOMIETSSA is not responsible for currency conversion losses.

Section 09

EU Consumer Rights

This Refund Policy is intended to comply with applicable EU consumer protection legislation, including EU Directive 2011/83/EU on consumer rights, and does not diminish any statutory rights you may have under the laws of your country of residence.

EU consumers have the right to lodge complaints with their national consumer protection authority if they believe their statutory rights have been violated. EU residents may also use the European Commission's Online Dispute Resolution platform.

Where applicable law grants consumers more generous refund or cancellation rights than those described in this policy, those statutory rights shall prevail.
